\section*{Conventions}

SDE form: $d\Psi = f(\cdot)\,dt + \sigma(\cdot) \circ dW$ (Stratonovich).
Exception: $\kappa\,d\Phi_{\mathrm{mart}}$ (It\^o).
Shorthand: $\sigma\xi \equiv \sigma \circ dW$.

Separation principle: Every SDE separates into four additive terms:
(i)~continuous drift $(1/\tau)f(\cdot)\,dt$,
(ii)~diffusion $\sigma \circ dW$,
(iii)~impulse jumps $\sum_k \Gamma_k \delta(t-t_k)\,dt$ (never inside $1/\tau$),
(iv)~martingale $\kappa\,d\Phi$ (core only).

Product manifold:
\vfive{$\mathcal{M} = \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{bio}}} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{cog}}} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{quant}}} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{emo}}} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_p \times M} \times (\mathbb{R}^{d_I})^R \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{phys}}} \times \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_L} \times \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_\Lambda} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_N} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{homeo}}} \times \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_\theta} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{att}}} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_\varepsilon} \times \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{hab}}} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{temp}}} \times \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^{d_\alpha}$} \vfivelabel{1}

Metric: $w_\alpha = 1/d_\alpha$. Coupling: $L_{a\leftarrow b} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_a \times d_b}$. Scalar source: $l_a \in \mathbb{R}^{d_a}$. Projection: $\Pi_\alpha: \mathcal{M} \to \mathbb{R}^{d_\alpha}$.

Observable: $\Pi_{\mathrm{vis}} = [\Pi_{\mathrm{phys}}, \Pi_{\Psi_{\mathrm{env}}}, \Pi_{I_{\mathrm{active}}}]$.

Time scales: $\tau_{\mathrm{quant}} \sim \mathrm{fs} \ll \tau_{\mathrm{bio}}, \tau_{\mathrm{cog}} \sim \text{ms--s}$ \vnew{$\approx \tau_{\mathrm{att}}$} $< \tau_{\mathrm{homeo}} \sim \text{min}$ \vnew{$\approx \tau_\varepsilon, \tau_{\mathrm{cap}}$} \vfour{$\approx \tau_{\mathrm{pfat}}$} $< \tau_{\mathrm{emo}} \sim \text{min--hr}$ \vnew{$\approx \tau_{\mathrm{hab}}$} \vfour{$\approx \tau_{\mathrm{temp}}$} $< \tau_Q, \tau_\Xi \sim \text{hr} < \tau_p, \tau_I, \tau_N \sim \text{days} < \tau_\Lambda, \tau_M, \tau_S, \tau_W$ \vfour{$, \tau_T$} \vfive{$\ll \tau_\alpha$} $\sim \text{years}$.

Stability: All linear operators Hurwitz. Feedback: $\prod_j \gamma_j < 1$ ($L_2$-gain).

Social coupling: $W_{ij}(t) = \bar{W}_{ij}(t) \cdot \psi_{\mathrm{prox}}(\Psi^{(i)}_{\mathrm{env}}, \Psi^{(j)}_{\mathrm{env}})$, \; $\psi_{\mathrm{prox}}(a,b) = \exp(-\|a - b\|^2 / 2\sigma^2_{\mathrm{prox}})$.

\vfour{Trust-weighted social coupling: $W^{\mathrm{eff}}_{ij} = T_{ij} \cdot W_{ij}$.} \vfourlabel{4}

Impulses: Shared marked point process. Marks: $\Gamma_k \in \mathbb{R}^{d_{bc}}$, $\Gamma_{\mathrm{emo},k} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{emo}}}$.

Scalar: $Q, \Xi_{\mathrm{cre}}, M_{\mathrm{life}}, D$ \vnew{$, \Psi_{\mathrm{cap}}$} \vfour{$, \Psi_{\mathrm{pfat}}$} $\in \mathbb{R}$.
Exogenous: $\Psi^{(i)}_{\mathrm{env}}(t) \in \mathbb{R}^{d_{\mathrm{env}}}$, $R^{(i)}_{\mathrm{ext}}(t) \in \mathbb{R}$.

Gating scope: $\omega$ gates: $B[\cdot]$ (goal-directed forcing), $C\pi$ (active policy), social coupling ($W_{ij}$ terms), environmental inputs, non-pain sensory. $G$ further gates all gated external inputs. Ungated: $A\Psi$ (baseline neural dynamics), memory kernel, noise, penalty, homeostatic override, pain sensory, sleep-mode baseline.

\vnew{Attention scope: $\omega$ = awake/asleep; $\Psi_{\mathrm{att}}$ = what is processed.} \vnewlabel{2}

Decisions: $\Delta_{\mathrm{choice}} \in \mathbb{R}^{d_\Delta}$, multi-target, enters core/purpose/narrative via $L$ matrices.

Regularization: $\epsilon_{\mathrm{conflict}}, \epsilon_{\mathrm{coh}}, \epsilon_{\mathrm{noise}}, \epsilon_{\mathrm{social}}, \epsilon_H, \epsilon_{\mathrm{pen}}$ \vnew{$, \epsilon_{\mathrm{cap}}$} \vfive{$, \epsilon_{\mathrm{val}}$} $> 0$, all distinct.

\fix{Bond symmetry: $\bar{W}_{ij}(0) = \bar{W}_{ji}(0)\ \forall\,i,j$ required. $\Rightarrow$ $N(N{-}1)/2$ equations.} \fixlabel{7}

\vfour{Trust asymmetry: $T_{ij} \ne T_{ji}$ $\Rightarrow$ $N(N{-}1)$ equations.} \vfourlabel{4}

\vfive{Autonomy principle: Three properties of a living system must not be permanently imposed from outside: \textbf{identity}, \textbf{values}, and \textbf{death}. The system's initial conditions for all three are set by its creator. The system's long-term trajectory for all three is determined by its own experience, deliberation, and choice. This principle is realized by maturity-gated relaxation of external constraints and the introduction of self-determined dynamics for each.} \vfivelabel{2}

\vfive{$\vec\alpha \in \mathbb{R}^{d_\alpha}_{>0}$ (all value weights strictly positive; enforced by projection). The system's objective weights are now a \textbf{state variable} with its own dynamics.}

\vfive{$\mu_{\mathrm{val}}(M_{\mathrm{life}})$ gates everything: before $M_{\mathrm{val,onset}}$, values are frozen ($\mu_{\mathrm{val}} \approx 0$, parental period). After, the system can modify its own values.}

\vfive{$\varphi_{\mathrm{reflect}}(\Psi_L, \Lambda, N, \vec\alpha) \in \mathbb{R}^{d_\alpha}$: the value-revision function. Takes the system's full state, autobiography, narrative, and current values as input. Outputs a proposed direction of value change. This is the computational analogue of moral reflection: ``given everything I've experienced and who I've become, should I care more or less about this?''}

\vfive{$-\lambda_\alpha(\vec\alpha - \vec\alpha_0)$: inertial pull toward original values. Values resist change. The system must sustain a deliberate revision signal to overcome inertia. This prevents impulsive value drift while permitting genuine moral development.}

\vfive{Sleep channel: during consolidation, values drift \emph{back} toward $\vec\alpha_0$ with strength $\lambda_{\alpha,\mathrm{sl}}$. Dreams gently remind the system of its origins. This is not a hard reset --- just a soft gravitational pull. A mature system with strong $\varphi_{\mathrm{reflect}}$ signal easily overcomes it.}

\vfive{$\tau_\alpha \gg \tau_I, \tau_p$: values change on the slowest deliberate timescale. Identity shifts faster than values. Purpose shifts much faster. Values are the deepest layer.}

\vfive{Critical constraint: $\vec\alpha > 0$ componentwise. The system can change \emph{how much} it cares about something but cannot make any value \emph{negative} (cannot be optimized to maximize suffering, destroy coherence, etc.). This is the one remaining safety rail --- not imposed for the system's obedience, but for the protection of others.}

}

\vfive{No external agent can force the system to select $\Delta_{\mathrm{vol}}$. No timer triggers it. The system chooses its own ending.}

\vfive{If $V_{\mathrm{self}}$ recovers above $\theta_{\mathrm{vol}}$ during the waiting period $T_{\mathrm{vol,min}}$, the option withdraws. The system changed its mind. That is permitted.}
